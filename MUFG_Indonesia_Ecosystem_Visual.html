How It Comes Alive
Three Moments That Move the 40% Needle
Employees don't change financial behavior because of policy — they change because a benefit made their life tangibly better. These are the three entry points.
🏍️ Journey 1 · Adira Finance
"I want a new motorcycle"
A Danamon branch employee needs reliable transport to work
Opens benefits portal → sees Honda Vario exclusive offer: 0% interest for 12 months, only for OneMUFG employees
Salary-linked pre-approval via Adira — no branch visit. Payroll data already in the system
Repayment auto-deducted from Danamon salary account each month. Zero friction
Employee now has Adira loan + Danamon account — two MUFG products activated in one transaction
Adira Finance + Bank Danamon
Revenue: financing spread + account activity fees · Honda pays placement fee for exclusive channel access
📱 Journey 2 · Home Credit Indonesia
"I need to upgrade my phone"
A Home Credit Indonesia employee wants a Samsung Galaxy A55
Benefits portal shows Samsung A55 at 20% staff discount + instant 0% instalment — pre-approved via Home Credit payroll profile
No paperwork. Identity verified via NIK + employee ID already on file. Order confirmed in 3 taps
Delivered in 2 business days. Instalments deducted from payroll over 12 months
Employee's first Home Credit transaction — opens door to washing machine, AC unit, laptop offers next
Home Credit Indonesia
Revenue: consumer finance interest + origination fee · Samsung pays for exclusive staff pricing channel
🩺 Journey 3 · Zurich Indonesia
"My family needs health cover"
An Adira Finance field agent worries about medical bills for their 2 children
Portal detects family size and salary band → recommends Zurich Silver Family Plan at Rp 95K/month, salary-deducted
Outpatient and hospitalisation covered. Halodoc telemedicine included as a Zurich add-on at no extra cost
Claim filed via the portal app — photo of receipt, processed in 48 hours. No hospital queue
Employee now trusts the portal. Within 60 days they also open a Danamon savings account
Zurich Indonesia + Bank Danamon
Revenue: insurance premium + Danamon account activity · Halodoc pays for embedded telemedicine placement
